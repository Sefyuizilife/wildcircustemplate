About Us

A driving force of French cultural influence for 25 years, The Wild Circus creates, produces and offers shows touching your inner child.

Considered a contemporary circus influencer, The Wild Circus combines circus arts with music, dance, technology and theater, fulfilling the expectations of audiences seeking meaningful entertainment.

These creations have been acclaimed by thousands of viewers and have over 5,500 performances in some 550 cities around the world.
